ABOUT TRENT AND JON

Trent has been building houses since he helped his dad build an addition on their house in 1978. Since then he has been involved with project management, overseeing 50 people.

Since 2003 he has owned and operated his own construction company in Calgary doing work in residential, estate homes, and commercial. His knowledge in project management and team building, as well as the early stages of the construction process make him an integral part of Tree Island.

Jon has been working in the Calgary region construction industry since 1999, where he began as a concrete cribber, forming residential flatwork. From there he moved on to window installation, and finally Finishing Carpentry/ Cabinetmaking in the summer of 2003.

His extensive knowledge in the finishing stages of construction make him a valuable asset to the Tree Island team.
